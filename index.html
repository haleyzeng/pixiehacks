What is "Filtrify"?

Ever so jealous of those ombre purple-pink picturesque tumblr posts? We sure were. That's why we created Filtrify! No photoshop experience? Psh...no more worries! Thanks to our jealousy, now you can become Tumblr famous too!* You're welcome. Use our tool to add beautiful filters to your picture and post to Tumblr from our site directly.
